Common Shoes Questions

How do I find my correct shoe size?

We recommend measuring your feet at the end of the day when they're at their largest. Use our printable size guide or measure in centimeters from heel to longest toe.

Can I exchange for a different size?

Yes! We offer free size exchanges within 30 days of delivery. Shoes must be unworn with original packaging. Visit our returns page to initiate an exchange.

Why do my shoes feel tight?

Leather shoes often need 3-5 wears to break in. If they're still uncomfortable after this period, you may need a wider width or half size up.

How can I tell if my shoes are authentic?

Genuine Noulika shoes have: a unique serial number inside, consistent stitching, and our logo embossed on the sole. Verify using our authenticity checker.

I received damaged/defective shoes

We're sorry to hear that! Please contact us within 14 days with photos of the issue. We'll send replacements or issue a refund for manufacturing defects.

Shoe Sizing Guide

Measure Your Feet

Stand on paper, mark heel and longest toe, then measure in cm. Compare to our size chart.

Consider Socks

Measure with the socks you'll wear. Leave about 1cm space at the toe for comfort.

Width Matters

We offer standard (B) and wide (D) widths. Measure the widest part of your foot.

Shoe Care Guide

Leather Shoes

Use cedar shoe trees to maintain shape. Clean with damp cloth, then apply conditioner. Waterproof before first wear.

Fabric Sneakers

Spot clean with mild soap. Stuff with paper when drying. Use protective spray for stains.

Weather Protection

Apply waterproofing treatments seasonally. Avoid wearing suede in rain. Dry wet shoes naturally.

Rotation

Rotate shoes every other day to allow them to air out and maintain their shape longer.
