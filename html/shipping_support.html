Common Shipping Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days after processing. Express shipping options are available at checkout for faster delivery (1-2 business days). Processing time is usually 1-2 business days.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship worldwide. International delivery times vary by destination but typically range from 7-14 business days. Additional customs fees may apply depending on your country's regulations.

What should I do if my package is damaged?

Please contact us immediately with photos of the damaged package and items. We'll work quickly to resolve the issue, either by sending replacements or issuing a refund.

My package is late - what can I do?

First, check your tracking information using our Order Tracking page. If your package is significantly delayed beyond the estimated delivery date, please contact us with your order number.

What's your return shipping policy?

We offer free returns within 30 days of delivery for domestic orders. International returns are subject to shipping costs. Please initiate returns through your account or contact us for assistance.

Need to track your order?

Visit our dedicated order tracking page to check the status of your shipment in real-time.
