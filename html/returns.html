Returns & Exchanges

Our Hassle-Free Return Policy

We want you to love everything you buy from us. If you're not completely satisfied, you can return most items within 30 days of delivery.

Return Policy Highlights

30-Day Window

You have 30 days from the delivery date to return your item for a refund or exchange.

Original Condition

Items must be unused, unworn, and in their original packaging with all tags attached.

Full Refunds

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method, excluding shipping costs.

How to Return an Item

Initiate Your Return

Log into your account and go to "Order History." Select the item(s) you wish to return and follow the prompts to generate your return label.

Start Return Process

Package Your Item

Place the item in its original packaging with all tags still attached. Include any free gifts that came with your order.

Attach Return Label

Securely attach the provided return label to your package. If original packaging is unavailable, use a sturdy box to prevent damage.

Ship Your Return

Drop off your package at any authorized shipping location. You'll receive an email when we receive and process your return.

Important Exceptions

Some items cannot be returned for hygiene or safety reasons:

  • Cosmetics and beauty products that have been opened or used
  • Underwear, swimwear, and hosiery (unless defective)
  • Personal care items
  • Custom or personalized items
  • Final sale items marked as non-returnable

For hygienic reasons, we cannot accept returns of pierced jewelry, even if unworn.

Exchange Policy

We're happy to exchange items for a different size or color if available. Exchanges follow the same process as returns:

Request an Exchange

Select "Exchange" instead of "Return" when initiating your return. Choose the new item you'd like to receive.

Ship Your Item

Follow the same packaging and shipping instructions as a standard return.

Receive Your New Item

We'll ship your exchange as soon as we receive your return. You'll be notified via email with tracking information.

Note: If the exchange item costs more than your original purchase, you'll need to pay the difference. If it costs less, we'll refund the difference.

Refund Information

Processing Time

Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after we receive your return. It may take additional time for your bank to post the credit.

Shipping Costs

Original shipping fees are non-refundable. Return shipping is free for defective or incorrect items.

Payment Methods

Refunds are issued to the original payment method. For PayPal and credit card payments, please allow 5-10 business days for the refund to appear.

Need Help With Your Return?

Our customer service team is happy to assist with any questions about returns or exchanges.
