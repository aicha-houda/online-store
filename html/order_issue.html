Order Issues Support

Having trouble with your order?

We're here to help you resolve any issues with your Noulika Store purchase. Below you'll find solutions to common order problems.

Order Not Received

If your order hasn't arrived within the expected delivery time:

  1. Check your shipping confirmation email for tracking information
  2. Track your package using the link provided
  3. If the tracking shows delivered but you haven't received it, check with neighbors or your building management
  4. Contact our support team if the issue persists

Wrong Item Received

If you received an item different from what you ordered:

  1. Keep all original packaging and tags
  2. Take photos of the incorrect item received
  3. Contact our support team with your order number and photos
  4. We'll arrange for return shipping and send the correct item

Damaged or Defective Item

If your item arrived damaged or isn't working properly:

  1. Take clear photos of the damage or defect
  2. Keep all original packaging
  3. Contact our support team within 14 days of delivery
  4. We'll provide a return label and replacement options

Order Cancellation

Need to cancel your order? Here's what to do:

  1. Check your order status - cancellations are only possible before shipping
  2. If your order hasn't shipped, contact us immediately
  3. For shipped orders, you can refuse delivery or return the item
  4. Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after return receipt
