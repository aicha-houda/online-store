Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the loyalty program?

You can join automatically by creating an account on our website. All registered customers are enrolled in the Classic tier of our loyalty program.

How do I check my points balance?

Your current points balance is displayed in your account dashboard. You'll also receive monthly statements via email.

Do points expire?

No, your points never expire as long as your account remains active (at least one purchase every 12 months).

How do I redeem my points?

At checkout, you'll see an option to apply your points balance. You can choose how many points to redeem based on your available balance.

Can I combine points with other promotions?

Yes! Points can be used alongside most promotions and discount codes for maximum savings.
