How to Measure Yourself

For accurate measurements, use a soft tape measure and follow these steps:

Bust

Measure around the fullest part of your bust, keeping the tape measure horizontal and slightly loose.

Waist

Measure around your natural waistline (where you normally wear pants), keeping one finger between the tape and your body.

Hips

Stand with feet together and measure around the fullest part of your hips, about 8 inches below your waist.

Fit by Product Type

Dresses & Tops

Our dresses and tops generally fit true to size. If between sizes or wanting a looser fit, consider sizing up. For fitted styles, your exact measurements will provide the best fit.

Bottoms & Jeans

Bottoms are designed to fit at your natural waist. Check both waist and hip measurements. Our jeans have 2% stretch - size down if you prefer a tighter fit.
