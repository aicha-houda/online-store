Common Clothing Questions

How do I find my correct size?

We provide detailed sizing charts for each clothing item. Measure yourself with a soft tape measure and compare to our size guides. When in doubt between sizes, we recommend sizing up for comfort.

Why does my item look different than the photo?

Colors may vary slightly due to monitor settings. Fabric texture and drape may appear different when worn versus on a model. Check the product description for exact fabric content and measurements.

Can I exchange for a different size?

Yes! We offer free size exchanges within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, with tags attached. Visit our returns page to initiate an exchange.

How can I tell if my item is authentic?

All Noulika clothing comes with an authenticity tag and holographic security sticker. You can verify your item using the unique code on our authenticity checker.

I received a damaged/defective clothing item

We're sorry to hear that! Please contact us within 14 days of receipt with photos of the issue. We'll send a replacement or issue a refund for any manufacturing defects.

Clothing Care Guides

Proper care will keep your Noulika clothing looking its best. Follow these guidelines based on fabric type:

Cotton & Linen

  • Machine wash cold, gentle cycle
  • Use mild detergent
  • Tumble dry low or hang to dry
  • Iron on medium heat if needed
  • May shrink slightly first wash

Silk & Delicates

  • Hand wash in cold water
  • Use gentle soap made for delicates
  • Lay flat to dry, never wring
  • Iron on low with cloth barrier
  • Dry clean recommended for some items

Synthetics & Blends

  • Machine wash cold
  • Turn inside out before washing
  • Use mesh laundry bag for protection
  • Line dry or tumble dry low
  • Avoid high heat to prevent pilling
