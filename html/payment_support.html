Need help with payments, refunds, or billing questions? We're here to help you resolve any payment-related issues.

Common Payment Questions

My payment was declined

This could happen for several reasons: insufficient funds, incorrect card details, or your bank may have blocked the transaction. Please verify your payment information and try again, or contact your bank for more details.

I was charged but didn't receive the product/service

Sometimes processing can take a few minutes. If you haven't received confirmation within 24 hours, please contact our support team with your transaction details.

How do I get a refund?

Refund requests should be submitted through our support system. Please include your order number and reason for the refund. Standard processing time is 5-7 business days.

When will my credit card be charged?

Your card is charged immediately when you place an order. For pre-orders, we charge when the item ships.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption to protect your payment information. We don't store your full credit card details on our servers.
