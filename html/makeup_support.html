Common Makeup Questions

How do I choose the right foundation shade?

Test foundation on your jawline in natural light. The perfect shade disappears into your skin. Consider your undertone (warm, cool, or neutral) for best results.

Why doesn't my makeup last all day?

Prep skin with primer, use long-wear formulas, and set with powder and setting spray. Oily skin may need blotting papers and touch-ups throughout the day.

Can I return opened makeup products?

For hygiene reasons, we can only accept returns of unopened, sealed makeup in original packaging within 30 days. We recommend checking shades in-store or ordering samples first.

How often should I replace my makeup?

Mascara: 3-6 months, Liquid foundation: 6-12 months, Powder products: 1-2 years, Lipstick: 1-2 years. Replace sooner if texture, smell, or color changes.

What if I have a reaction to a product?

Discontinue use immediately. For severe reactions, seek medical attention. Contact our support team with product details and batch number for assistance.

Makeup Application Guide

Prep Your Skin

Start with clean, moisturized skin. Use primer to create a smooth base and help makeup last longer. Let skincare absorb before applying makeup.

Application Tools

Use clean brushes, sponges, or fingers. Damp beauty blenders give sheer coverage, brushes offer more precision, and fingers warm product for better blending.

Build Gradually

Apply products in thin layers, building coverage as needed. Start with foundation, then concealer, powder, blush/bronzer, eyes, and lips last.

Products for Your Skin Type

Choose formulas that work with your skin type for best results:

Oily Skin

Matte or oil-free foundations, powder blushes, and long-wear formulas that control shine

Dry Skin

Creamy, hydrating formulas with luminous finishes. Avoid powders that can emphasize dryness

Combination

Balance matte and luminous products. Use different formulas on different areas as needed

Sensitive

Fragrance-free, hypoallergenic products. Patch test new items before full application

Mature Skin

Lightweight, hydrating formulas that don't settle into lines. Cream products often work best

Proper Makeup Storage

Avoid Heat & Sunlight

Store away from windows and heat sources which can degrade products

Keep Dry

Store in dry areas - bathroom humidity can breed bacteria in products

Seal Tightly

Close lids securely to prevent drying out and contamination

Clean Tools

Wash brushes weekly and sponges after each use to prevent bacteria buildup
